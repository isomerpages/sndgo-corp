<p>Venturing into a new business or running an existing one can be fraught
with challenges. However, there are also opportunities as Singapore and
the world undergoes a digital transformation.</p>
<p>So whether you’re just starting out or looking to expand your operations,
we want to help you along this entrepreneurial journey. Below are some
resources you should check out.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="e-adviser banner" src="/images/resources/e-adviser.jpg">
</div>
<h2><a href="https://www.imda.gov.sg/accreditation" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Accreditation@SGD</a></h2>
<p>Are you a Singapore-based, high-growth infocomm media company or start-up
with a promising and innovative solution? If so, this is for you. Accreditation@SGD
seeks to help open up more opportunities for your products and solutions
to be showcased to a wider audience – at home and abroad.</p>
<h2><a href="https://www.imda.gov.sg/how-we-can-help/smes-go-digital/advanced-digital-solutions" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Advanced Digital Solutions (ADS)</a></h2>
<p>The Advanced Digital Solutions (ADS) was announced as part of the Resilience
Budget 2020 to help enterprises deepen their digital capabilities, strengthen
business continuity measures, and build longer term resilience. ADS supports
the adoption of advanced technologies (e.g., AI, Robotics, Blockchain and
Internet of Things), and integrated digital solutions (e.g., B2B solutions
that integrate inventory management, e-invoicing, and digital payments)
that address common enterprise-level challenges at scale.</p>
<h2><a href="https://www.aisingapore.org/aiap/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">AI Apprenticeship Programme (AIAP)</a></h2>
<p>Do you aspire to become an engineer, developer or consultant in the artificial
intelligence (AI) sector? Join this 9-month, full-time training programme
designed by industry practitioners focusing on AI and machine-learning
systems. More details can be found on AI.SG’s website.</p>
<h2><a href="https://www.businessgrants.gov.sg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Business Grants Portal</a></h2>
<p>For businesses looking for Government grants, this one-stop portal can
help you find and apply for the grants you need quickly. The website is
currently in beta and more grants from different agencies will be introduced
in phases.</p>
<h2><a href="https://www.enterprisesg.gov.sg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Enterprise Singapore</a></h2>
<p>Calling all Singapore companies looking to build their capabilities, innovate
and/or go international – Enterprise Singapore wants to lend a helping
hand.</p>
<p>Whether you’re a start-up or a high-growth business, ESG will lend its
support according to your company’s growth stage, the industry and the
overseas market you’re looking to expand to.</p>
<h2><a href="https://www.gobusiness.gov.sg/gov-assist/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">GoBusiness Gov Assist</a></h2>
<p>With more than 100 Government schemes available to support local companies,
it can be mind-boggling for businesses to navigate and find what’s applicable
to them.</p>
<p>GoBusiness Gov Assist provides information on the various assistance options
available as you grow your business – all in one place! Have more queries?
An e-Adviser is on hand to help too.</p>
<h2><a href="https://www.ihis.com.sg/HealthX/Pages/home.aspx" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">HealthX</a></h2>
<p>Have a promising idea or solution that harnesses tech to transform Singapore’s
healthcare or improve public health?</p>
<p>An innovation hub to bring HealthTech ideas to reality, HealthX provides
multiple pathways to support you (public healthcare institutions/agencies,
and industry partners), no matter which stage you are at in your smart
health innovation journey. Whether you have an idea on paper, are looking
to develop or test your prototype, or seeking support to scale your innovation,
HealthX provides the innovation platforms and enablers to help inspire
health through innovation.</p>
<h2><a href="https://www.tech.gov.sg/files/media/media-releases/2017/02/Annex%20B%20InnoLeap%20Factsheet.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">InnoLeap (160kb)</a></h2>
<p>Innoleap is a match-making programme that brings together public sector
agencies with problems they’re looking to solve, with commercial entities
that may have ready solutions to tackle them. The programme enables the
partners to collaborate and work together on developing the required tools.</p>
<h2><a href="https://www.mas.gov.sg/news/media-releases/2016/mas-establishes-fintech-innovation-lab" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Looking Glass @ MAS</a></h2>
<p>"Looking Glass", a FinTech Innovation Lab at the Monetary Authority of
Singapore (MAS) seeks to help financial institutions, start-ups or technology
vendors that have an idea to develop. The platform supports the experimentation
and deployment of FinTech solutions; and facilitates consultations with
industry experts on areas such as legal, regulatory and business-related
matters.</p>
<h2><a href="https://business.myinfo.gov.sg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">MyInfo Business</a></h2>
<p>MyInfo Business helps business owners save time and effort when submitting
Government-verified data such as corporate profile, financial performance
and ownership information.</p>
<p>Singapore-registered business owners can log in through Corppass, give
consent and have their data pre-filled for participating services. More
B2B services will be introduced and developer tools will also soon be made
available to facilitate businesses' technical implementation.</p>
<h2><a href="https://www.imda.gov.sg/impixel#2" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">PIXEL</a></h2>
<p>PIXEL is a gathering space for entrepreneurs, freelancers and makers to
acquire new skills, generate ideas, experiment, collaborate and build prototypes.
It hosts mixers, workshops and masterclasses so that the community can
come together to learn and network. It can also be used as an incubator
or a co-working space.</p>
<h2><a href="https://www.sginnovate.com/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">SGInnovate</a></h2>
<p>SGInnovate supports entrepreneurial people who want to build products
and companies in science and tech through Singapore's innovation ecosystem.</p>
<p>By marrying human and investment capital, SGInnovate is committed to adding
value to the local start-up ecosystem.</p>
<h2><a href="https://www.imda.gov.sg/smesgodigital" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">SMEs Go Digital Programme</a></h2>
<p>SMEs are the core of Singapore's economy and we want to make sure they
have the resources to build more robust digital capabilities. With SMEs
Go Digital, businesses can participate in this programme to leverage various
support schemes to facilitate their digital transformation.</p>
<p>The programme will cover business needs including productivity tools such
as accounting software, digital ordering and payment, fleet management
as well as cybersecurity, data protection and data analytics.</p>
<h2><a href="https://www.startupsg.gov.sg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Startup SG Tech</a></h2>
<p>Start-ups with proprietary technology solutions or are seeking support
for Proof-of-Concept (POC) and Proof-of-Value (POV), can approach Startup
SG Tech to seek early-stage funding for commercialisation of proprietary
tech ideas!</p>
<h2><a href="https://www.a-star.edu.sg/i2r/partnerships/small-and-medium--sized-enterprises-(smes)" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Technology Adoption Programme (TAP)</a></h2>
<p>SMEs, are you ready to tap on TAP?</p>
<p>The programme supports collaboration among public sector research institutes,
private sector tech providers, Institutes of Higher Learning, Trade Associations
and Chambers (TACs) and private sector system integrators, to identify
and translate new technologies into real-world solutions.</p>
<h2><a href="http://www.mas.gov.sg/Singapore-Financial-Centre/Smart-Financial-Centre/Setting-up-your-Business.aspx" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">The Financial Sector Technology and Innovation Scheme</a></h2>
<p>The Financial Sector Technology and Innovation scheme was launched to
support innovation in FinTech and create a vibrant ecosystem with the following
:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Proof-of-concept projects: To support the early-stage development of innovative
projects</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Institution-level projects: To catalyse the development of innovative
solutions that have the potential to promote growth, efficiency or competitiveness</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Industry-wide projects: To support the building of industry-wide technology
infrastructure or utility that is required for the delivery of new, integrated
services</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Innovation centres: To attract financial institutions to set up their
innovation labs in Singapore</p>
</li>
</ul>
