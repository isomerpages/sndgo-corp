# About Smart Nation and Digital Government Group


The Smart Nation and Digital Government Office (SNDGO), under the Prime Ministerâ€™s Office (<a href="https://www.pmo.gov.sg/">PMO</a>), plans and prioritises key Smart Nation projects and drives the digital transformation of our Government.
